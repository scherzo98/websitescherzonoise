Experimental / Post-digital Lutherie

Handcrafted instruments and sound objects that bridge analog materiality and digital processing. Each instrument is designed as a hybrid system where acoustic resonance meets electronic intervention.

Resonant Body #1

A feedback-based instrument using piezoelectric transducers and resonant wooden chambers. The instrument creates self-oscillating drones that can be shaped through physical pressure and position changes.

Contact Microphone Array

A modular system of contact microphones designed for amplified object manipulation. Each channel is processed independently, creating spatialized textures from everyday materials.
