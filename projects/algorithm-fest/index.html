{PinkNoise.ar} Algorithm Music Fest

{PinkNoise.ar} Algorithm Music Fest is an annual festival dedicated to algorithmic and live coding music. As co-curator and performer, I have been involved in organizing and presenting works that explore the intersection of code, sound, and performance.

The festival brings together international artists working with SuperCollider, TidalCycles, FoxDot, and custom live coding environments. Each edition features performances, workshops, and discussions about the future of computer music and the democratization of electronic sound through open-source tools.
