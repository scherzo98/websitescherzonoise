Sinfonía Biótica

Sinfonía Biótica is an immersive VR experience that transforms biological data into sonic ecosystems. Working as Senior Audio Designer, I developed a procedural audio system that translates real-time biometric signals into evolving soundscapes, creating a symbiotic relationship between the user and virtual organisms.

The project explores how sound can mediate our understanding of living systems, using spatial audio to create a sense of presence within microscopic biological processes. Each session generates a unique sonic narrative based on the user's physiological responses.
