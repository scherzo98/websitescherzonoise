Mareo Migrante MM

Mareo Migrante MM is a collaborative performance work that brings together contemporary dance and live electroacoustic composition. Developed in partnership with choreographers and dancers, the piece explores themes of displacement, memory, and bodily memory through an immersive sonic environment.

The electroacoustic score is performed live, responding in real-time to the dancers' movements through motion-capture sensors. The result is a constantly shifting sonic landscape that mirrors the physical and emotional states of the performers, creating a dialogue between flesh and frequency.
