Interactive Sound Installation

Liminal Gesture

Liminal Gesture is an interactive sound installation that explores the boundaries between physical movement and sonic response. Through custom sensors and real-time audio processing, the installation translates corporeal gestures into evolving soundscapes, creating a dialogue between the body and the acoustic environment.

The work investigates the threshold spaces where intention meets intuition, where the conscious control of movement dissolves into reactive improvisation. Each interaction generates a unique sonic fingerprint, never to be repeated exactly.
